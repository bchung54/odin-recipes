Egg Fried Rice

Description

Simple egg fried rice for a quick meal that can incorporate any leftover vegetables or proteins.

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • Eggs
  • Rice, cooked & cooled
  • Soy sauce
  • Frozen vegetables, peas & carrots

Steps

  1. Grab a wok and place on medium-high heat. Add vegetable oil.
  2. Add eggs into the wok and scramble. Be sure to keep eggs soft and fluffy by quickly moving it around.
  3. Add rice. Mix it in with the egg and be sure to break up large lumps.
  4. Once the rice is mixed in, add the vegetables.
  5. Add soy sauce down the sides of the wok to heat it up as it adds flavor to the rice.
