Tiramisu

Description

Fairly modern Italian dessert paired down to its essentials. Ladyfingers covered in a creamy mixture of eggs and cream, all topped with cocoa.

Ingredients

  • Ladyfinger biscuits
  • Egg yolks
  • Heavy cream
  • Sugar
  • Mascarpone cheese
  • Coffee
  • Cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Make coffee ahead of time and let cool. Take mascarpone out of the fridge ahead of time to let it loosen up.
  2. Place egg yolks and half the sugar into a mixing bowl. Whisk for about 5 minutes over a pot of simmering water.
  3. Add mascarpone into the beaten mixture and keep whisking until there are no more lumps of cheese.
  4. In a separate bowl, whip the cream until soft peaks form (about 5 minutes).
  5. Fold the two mixtures together until well incorporated.
  6. Dip ladyfingers into the cold coffee for 1-2 seconds on each side. Layer them in the dish or tray that you will use to make the tiramisu.
  7. Cover the layer of ladyfingers with half of the egg and cream mixture. Spread evenly and add a thin layer of cocoa powder.
  8. Repeat last two steps to add another layer of ladyfingers and cream.
  9. Cover with cling wrap and a lid. Place in fridge to let it firm up overnight.
